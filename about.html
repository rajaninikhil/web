Every Prayer is Answered...

Parimal Mandir began its journey in 1947 as Gandhi Flour Mills & Perfumery Works, founded by Late Sunku Gundaiah Setty in Bangalore. With a vision to deliver world-class products in unconventional packaging, he set the foundation for a brand built on quality, value, and trust. Today, Parimal Mandir stands as a global name in incense, spreading soulful aromas to over 45 countries.
